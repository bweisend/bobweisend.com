HOW TO PLAY "SIMON SAYS"

It's just like the Simon game:

  1. Click the 'START GAME' button to begin.
  2. One of the four colors will randomly light up.
  3. Click that color.
  4. Then a second color will be added to the sequence.
  5. Click both colors in the correct sequence.
  6. This continues until you make a mistake. How long can you last?
